Thumb Rules

Low Risk (More Gold, Less Market Exposure)
50-50 Rule
• 50% Mutual Funds, 50% Gold
• More protection, but lower growth potential.
High Risk (More Growth-Oriented)
80-20 Rule
• 80% Mutual Funds, 20% Gold
• Best for aggressive investors who want higher returns.
Moderate Risk (Balanced Growth & Stability)
70-30 Rule
70% Mutual Funds, 30% Gold
Mutual Funds for long-term wealth creation (12-15% returns).
Gold for stability during market downturns.
